War/Peace Triptych

Benjamin Winans

War/Peace is an ongoing project, uniting art and philosophy with hope to help individuals see beyond their personal views, and broaden their thinking.

War/Peace is the profound realization that we have a choice in what we consume and assimilate into our minds and lives. It is a creative and collaborative project which intends to serve as a thoughtful reminder to slow down so we can allow meaningful dialogue to emerge about what’s happening around us in our communities and abroad.
